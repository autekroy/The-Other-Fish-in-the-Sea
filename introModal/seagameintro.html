Let's Begin!

When you are on the island, you will see some treasure boxes that look like this:

Try to collect as many treasure boxes as possible by clicking on them.
Each treasure box you collect gives you one extra life point that you will probably need when you fight monsters under the sea.

Use W, S, A, D, I and K keys to control your motion. Once you get under the sea, you will see many different monsters.

Avoid them or kill them using a sword that you can find in sword boxes floating around you.

From time to time, you may also see some Magic Mushroom boxes.

Eating the magic mushrooms in the box makes you transparent so that the monster cannot see you even if you hit them.

By the way, the fish swimming around you are harmless.

Don't be afraid of them! Remember, when you are under the sea, you collect boxes by bumping into them, not clicking them like when you are on an island. You will need to conquer a few islands before you get to the final destination. You can talk to the person you find on each island by lightly bumping into them. Have fun!
